FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When should my child start working with Noble Vision?
The earlier the better. Ideally from Grade 10, so we have the full high school period to build the right academic profile, extracurricular record, and test preparation strategy. However, students at any stage — including university — can benefit significantly from our guidance.
Do you only work with students interested in US medical schools?
Our primary focus is on the US medical school pathway (pre-med, MCAT, AMCAS). However, we also assist students exploring medical programs in other countries. Our general academic planning, personal statement coaching, and interview preparation services are broadly applicable.
What does the initial consultation involve?
The initial 30-minute consultation is free of charge and involves a conversation about your current academic standing, goals, timeline, and questions. Based on this, we propose a personalised service recommendation. There is no obligation to proceed.
Are consultations available online?
Yes. All our consultations and ongoing sessions are available online via video call, making us accessible to students and families worldwide. In-person consultations may also be arranged for those in the Texas area.
What makes Noble Vision different from other consultancies?
Noble Vision is founded and led by Dr. Natarajan Venkatayan, MD — an active physician with over 20 years of clinical, GME leadership, and academic experience. Our guidance is rooted in deep, first-hand knowledge of how medical education works, what admissions committees look for, and what it takes to succeed as a physician. This is not generic consulting — it is mentorship from someone who has lived the journey.
How much do your services cost?
Pricing is tailored to the level of support you choose and the duration of engagement. We offer packages from focused application support to full multi-year journey programs. Please contact us or register above for a detailed proposal based on your specific needs and goals.
Can Noble Vision guarantee admission to medical school?
No ethical consultancy can guarantee admission — and we will not make that promise. What we guarantee is that you will be maximally prepared: a strong application, a compelling personal statement, thorough interview readiness, and the academic and personal profile needed to compete at the highest level. Our goal is to give you the best possible chance.
